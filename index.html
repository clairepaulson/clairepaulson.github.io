Welcome!

Hi, I’m Claire! I’m an instructional designer who specializes in creating learner-centric, engaging, and effective online learning experiences. Though I’ve worked in a higher ed context for much of my career, my work has spanned a wide range of subject areas, tools, and audiences. My unique background in cognitive science and educational program evaluation means I’m always striving to ground my work in evidence: I keep an eye on learning sciences research and pay close attention to learner performance to improve my designs. Check out my resume below or explore this site to learn more about me and my work!

Projects

Below is a selection of significant projects I’ve worked on in the past several years. Click on each card to learn more about the project.

Juvenile Remediation Curriculum

Worked with SMEs in juvenile justice and social work to design and develop a 10-module remediation curriculum designed for juvenile defendants found not competent to stand trial. Curriculum built in Articulate Rise with multiple Vyond and Storyline components and hosted on Canvas LMS.

Online MPA Migration

Led the migration of SOG’s online MPA program from an external vendor to an in-house setting. Work included template development, overseeing development and QA for all courses, developing new SOPs for online course creation and updates, and leading internal training and communications efforts.

APN Building Multidisciplinary Teams

Contracted with internal clients to develop a 13-module course designed for Aging and Adult Services professionals around North Carolina. Course built in DominKnow ONE with multiple Vyond components.

LELA Badging

Planned, designed, and developed a micro-credentialing program with complex learning pathways for the Local Elected Leaders Academy, a joint leadership training initiative of the School of Government, the North Carolina Association of County Commissioners, and the North Carolina League of Municipalities.

DataAware Program

Designed, planned, and coordinated a new research and training program in health data analytics for high school students, adapting existing curriculum for undergraduates and developing new curricular modules. Taught lessons in health technology and research ethics.
